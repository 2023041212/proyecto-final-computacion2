MI ENTREVISTA Y MI SENTIR AL ENTREVISTAR HABLANDO OTRO IDIOMA

La entrevista que hice fue simple para no incomodar al entrevistado, por lo que solo le hice 10 preguntas las cuales fueron
  • What's your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How old are you?
  • Why are you visiting Mexico city?
  • Have tou visited another places apart from the historic center of Mexico city?
  • How has your experience visiting these places?
  • What do you like the most about Mexico?
  • How many days have tou been here?
  • What was you first impression about Mexico?
  • What do you think about mexican people?
